# Documentation: TimeSnap for Jira

**Effortless Time Tracking and Monthly Timesheets**

## Overview

TimeSnap is a Forge-based Custom UI app designed to simplify how teams track their work within Jira. By providing a clean, intuitive interface, TimeSnap allows users to log hours, manage monthly timesheets, and monitor their capacity without leaving their Jira environment.

## Key Features

* **Monthly Timesheet Navigation:** Easily browse through past and current months to review or enter time.
* **Default Activity Logging:** Quick-entry fields for standard work activities to save time.
* **Custom Activity Tracking:** Add specific task names or project-specific activities dynamically.
* **Real-time Hour Counter:** Automatically calculates total hours logged and shows the remaining balance of a standard 40-hour work week.
* **Forge-Powered Security:** Built on Atlassian's Forge platform, ensuring your data never leaves the Atlassian ecosystem.

## Installation

1. Navigate to the **Atlassian Marketplace** within your Jira Cloud instance.
2. Search for **TimeSnap for Jira**.
3. Click **Get it now** to install the app.
4. Once installed, TimeSnap will be available in your Jira sidebar or Apps menu (depending on your configuration).

## How to Use

1. **Opening the App:** Open the TimeSnap project page or global page from the Jira menu.
2. **Logging Time:** Select the jira ticket. Enter the number of hours spent on your default activities.
3. **Reviewing Progress:** The "Total Hours" bar at the bottom will update instantly, helping you track your 40-hour goal.
