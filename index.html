Follow these simple steps to start tracking your Jira worklogs:

Step 1: Connect Jira

Authorize TimeSnap to access your Jira worklogs securely.

Step 2: Log Work

Use the app to log hours and tasks directly into Jira.

Step 3: Export & Analyze

Download timesheets as Excel or view dashboards for team insights.

Privacy & Data Usage

TimeSnap only reads Jira worklogs and stores minimal configuration data in Atlassian Forge Storage. No data is sent outside Atlassian.
